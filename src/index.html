Who am I?

Hello! Thank you for visiting my personal bio site. My name is Davis Lindell. I arrived in Nashville, TN by way of Louisville, KY in August of 2016.

Currently, I am a bartender a local establishment in midtown Nashville. Previously, I have been employeed at various positions within the hospitality industry, ranging from dishwasher to manager, in both front of house and back of house operations. In addition to this I have also worked as CNC operator and programmer at a woodworking mill.

Funnily enough, my first experience in computer programming was editing my own personal MySpace page 15+ years ago. I always thought it was so cool to make the most obnoxious color changing gifs be the background of my pages.\ Who knew that one day, all these years later I would be applying what I learned all those years ago and turning it into a career. If you were to have told me then that I would have found a passion in doing that, I would have said you were crazy. But here we are. Learning new things has always been a passion of mine. With the current job climate surrounding the technology industry here in Nashville getting into development just seems like the perfect move at the perfect time.
